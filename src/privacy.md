# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 9 July 2026*

**Data controller:** Marius Vasile VRÂNCIANU — [marius.v.vrancianu@gmail.com](mailto:marius.v.vrancianu@gmail.com)

This is a personal, non-commercial website. I collect as little data as possible; here is everything it processes:

**Hosting.** The site is served by GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., USA). Like any web server, GitHub receives your IP address in its server logs when it delivers pages to you ([GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)). Legal basis: my legitimate interest in serving a website (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

**Analytics — only with your consent.** If you click "Accept" on the cookie banner, Google Analytics (Google LLC, USA) sets cookies and collects usage data: pages visited, approximate location derived from your IP address, and device/browser information. I use it solely to see which posts people read. If you decline or ignore the banner, nothing loads and no data is sent to Google. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). Withdraw it any time via **Cookie settings** in the footer; analytics data is retained by Google for 14 months.

**Comments.** Posting a comment sends your nickname, optional email address, comment text, and time of posting to Cusdis (an open-source comment service, hosted on US infrastructure — [Cusdis privacy policy](https://cusdis.com/privacy-policy)). Your data is displayed with your comment and kept until you ask me to delete it. No account and no cookies are involved. Legal basis: your request to publish a comment (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

**Images.** Gallery and post images are delivered by the jsDelivr CDN, which receives your IP address to send you the files. Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

**Embedded videos.** Some posts embed YouTube videos in privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com). YouTube (Google) receives data about you only if you play a video.

**Local storage.** Your browser stores two values locally (theme choice, cookie-consent choice). They never leave your device and are not tracking.

**International transfers.** GitHub and Google are certified under the [EU-US Data Privacy Framework](https://www.dataprivacyframework.gov/), an EU adequacy decision. Cusdis stores data in the USA; by commenting you acknowledge this transfer.

**Your rights.** You can request access to, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data, object to processing, and withdraw consent — email me at the address above. You may also complain to a supervisory authority; in Romania that is [ANSPDCP](https://www.dataprotection.ro) (B-dul G-ral Gheorghe Magheru 28-30, Bucharest).

**No profiling.** This site makes no automated decisions and builds no profiles.
